Tuck

Chrome extension Shipped 2026 Free

If you run more than a handful of extensions, your Chrome toolbar turns into a cockpit. Tuck hides all of them behind one icon. Click it and you get a clean dropdown of every extension, each with a toggle to enable or disable it on the spot.

You can sort them into your own colour-coded folders, search by name, and flip everything off in one tap. The folders sync across all your Chromes, so nothing is ever lost.
