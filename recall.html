Recall

macOS app Shipped 2026 Free

Every project needs the same set of windows open: the right apps, the right files, the right Chrome profile with the right tabs. Setting that up by hand every morning is a tax. Recall saves a workspace once and brings the whole thing back in a single click.

It reopens the apps and files, launches the correct Chrome profiles and tabs, and then snaps every window back to the exact position you left it in. You go from a bare desktop to ready-to-work in one move.
