Freelancers

Freelancers and solo operators

Your business is you. Briefs, proposals, feedback rounds, unpaid invoices. These push the admin down so more of your week goes to the actual work. 8 prompts, all free to copy.

01

Messy client notes into a brief

Use this when the client sent three voice notes and half an idea, and you need a real brief before any work starts.

 → 02

Decode vague client feedback

Use this when the feedback says "make it pop" and you need to know what to actually change.

 → 03

Draft a proposal with my rates

Use this when the scope is roughly agreed and you need a clean proposal that uses your numbers, never invented ones.

 → 04

Push back on scope creep

Use this when the client asks for one more small thing and the small things are becoming the project.

 → 05

Chase an unpaid invoice politely

Use this when the invoice is overdue and you want your money without burning the relationship.

 → 06

Send updates before they ask

Use this when a project runs long and you want the client calm because they always know where things stand.

 → 07

Decline work without closing doors

Use this when a project is wrong for you but the relationship is worth keeping.

 → 08

Explain my work to the client

Use this when the work is good but the client needs the reasoning in words to feel safe saying yes.
