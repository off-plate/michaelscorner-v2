Best prompts for writing

8 prompts

Draft faster and still sound like yourself. These keep your voice and cut the filler. They also stop your text from reading like a machine wrote it, because readers can tell.

01

Rewrite this in my voice

Use this when you have a rough draft and want it to read the way you actually talk.

 → 02

Cut this until it is sharp

Use this when your text is too long and you can no longer see what to remove.

 → 03

Messy notes into a first draft

Use this when you have scattered notes and the blank page is the only thing between you and a draft.

 → 04

Strip the AI flavor out

Use this when a draft smells like AI and you need it to read human again.

 → 05

Make my English sound natural

Use this when English is not your first language and you want your text checked without changing who you are.

 → 06

Critique my draft, do not rewrite

Use this when you want to stay the writer and just need a sharp second pair of eyes.

 → 07

Ten titles, three worth keeping

Use this when the piece is done and the title is the last thing standing.

 → 08

Rewrite for a different reader

Use this when one text has to land with a second audience and a copy paste will not do it.
