How the score works

Each answer scores 0, 1 or 2, weighted by how much it matters. Frequency and whether the task is rules or judgment carry the most weight, because those decide whether a machine can follow it at all.

Three hard rules can override the score: a judgment call with expensive mistakes stays human, sensitive data caps you at assist-only, and rare tasks with slow setup never pay back. Those are not negotiable.
