How to read this number

The character count is exact. The token estimate is characters divided by the chars-per-token figure you set, so it is a rough guide by design, not a promise. A window has to hold your text plus the reply, which is why some room is reserved.

FITS means comfortable room. TIGHT means it squeezes in but risks truncation. SPLIT means break it into the number of parts shown.
