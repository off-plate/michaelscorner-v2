Prompt tightener

Paste a rambling prompt; it strips the filler it can prove is filler, flags the vague asks it cannot fix for you, and shows the word count drop. Deletion only. It never rewrites or adds a word.

Why deletion, not rewriting

    
If a tool rewrites your prompt, your meaning can drift and you would not notice. This one only removes exact filler phrases from a published list. Anything it is unsure about becomes a flag for you, not an edit. Text inside quotes or backticks is left alone.

    
A tight prompt is not a shorter prompt for its own sake. It is one where every word is doing a job.
