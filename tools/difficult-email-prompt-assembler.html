Why a builder, not a generator

    
The expensive work in a hard email is translation: turning what happened into words that get the outcome without burning the relationship. This tool does the structure. Your AI writes the draft from your facts. You read it and decide to send.

    
Nothing is generated on this page. Every line in the prompt came from an input you can see.
