What the score means

The score is hand-tuned so raw chatbot marketing copy lands above 60 and edited human copy lands under 20. It is a density measure: tells per word, floored so a two-line paste cannot spike. Every rule that ran is listed above, so the page cannot lie about what it counted.

A high score is not proof of anything. It means the text leans on patterns AI over-produces. Fix the flagged lines and the number drops.
